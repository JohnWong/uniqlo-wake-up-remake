ALARM MUSIC BY
CORNELIUS × YOKO KANNO

Cornelius/Keigo Oyamada

Began performing in 1993 as Cornelius.

To date, has released three albums worldwide.

In addition to his own work, has participated in a wide range of activities, including collaborations, remixes and producing with a large number of artists in Japan and abroad.

Nominated for a Grammy (Best Surround Sound Album) in the 51st Grammy Awards in the United States in 2009.

His passionate fan base spans the globe but is especially large in the US, Europe, and Japan.
www.cornelius-sound.com

Yoko Kanno

A composer. In addition to composing/producing for artists, she is involved in music across a genre-busting variety of areas such as commercials, anime, games, drama, and movies.

Has performed numerous times with the
Warsaw National Philharmonic Orchestra
and her compositions for anime titles such as Cowboy Bebop, Stand Alone Complex (S.A.C.) Solid State Society, and Macross are highly popular not only in Japan but also in the United States, Brazil, France, China, etc.

For the sound track of anime series Wolf’s Rain, collaborated with Brazil’s “Queen of Bossa Nova,” Joyce Moreno.

Yoko Kanno enjoys huge support from fans and artists in Japan and a variety of other countries, including a British artist Mika who cites her as his favorite arranger and producer in the world.
